About Me


My work is an obsession, so every day and every shoot is an opportunity to learn and to further develop my craft - discovering more about myself, and about those around.


I shoot digital, but I can give any kind of image, including film. Of course, this cannot be compared with analog shooting, but I try to convey the surroundings of the picture as much as possible through digital processing.


I am available for private portrait sessions, as well as events in the Pavlovsky Posad and Moscow region areas - though I would also be very interested in working on location elsewhere.


I'd love to hear from you, so feel free to contact me.


Thank you for taking time to look at my site and work; I hope to meet you in the near future.


- Ilia Vedmenko
